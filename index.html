Crystal Collector

How To Play:

  • To play the game you will be given a random number displayed in the box below.
  • You are presented with four (4) rare crystals. Hidden in the crystal are random numbers. You must click on it to reveal the number.
  • Players must click on the crystals to add up the total matching the random number dispalyed in the box below.
  • When your score matches the random number, you win.
  • When your score exceeds the random number, you lose.
  • The random number and hidden numbers within crystals will reset each time the game starts.
